# 🦉 BizLingo

A Duolingo-style game for learning business terms and concepts — built for new entrepreneurs.

**Play it:** https://hhara513.github.io/bizlingo/

- 🌍 Learn in **English, Español, or 日本語** (progress carries across languages)
- 📚 4 units · 12 lessons · 48 questions: business basics, marketing & sales, finance, strategy
- 🎮 XP, daily streaks, hearts, achievements, and four exercise types
- 💡 Hover or tap any underlined business term for an instant definition
- 📱 Installable as an app — open the link on your phone and choose **Add to Home Screen**
- ✈️ Works fully offline after the first visit; progress is saved on your device

Built as a single dependency-free HTML file. No accounts, no tracking, no backend.
